### 课程概述

- 课程目标
    - 学习计算机视觉相关的知识

    	- `Linear Algebra` / `Optimization` / `Geometry`
- 课程资源
    - 教材：[https://szeliski.org/Book/](https://szeliski.org/Book/)     
&nbsp;

- 课程计划
    - Basics. (Lec.02 – Lec.04)
    - Reconstruction. (Lec.05 – Lec.09) 
    - Understanding. (Lec.10 – Lec.12)
    - Synthesis. (Lec. 13 – Lec.14)
